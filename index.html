Bridging the Gap Between Potential and Opportunity

Future Forward SA helps young South Africans get the skills and confidence they need to start real careers. We run workshops, mentorship programs, and other initiatives that prepare youth for both the workplace and life.

Image: Helena Lopes, Unsplash

Our Programs

Digital Skills

Our 12-week Digital Skills program covers web literacy, basic coding (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), and hands-on projects like building a simple website. Students get guided practice, mentorship, and real-world examples to build confidence in technology.

Image: Christina @ wocintechchat.com, Unsplash

Soft Skills

The Soft Skills program teaches communication, teamwork, leadership, and workplace etiquette. Students practice real-life scenarios and learn how to present themselves confidently in professional settings.

Image: fauxels, Pexels

Career Readiness

Our Career Readiness program helps students craft CVs, prepare for interviews, and get mentorship from industry professionals. We also connect learners with potential employers through job placement opportunities.
